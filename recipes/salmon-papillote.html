Salmon Papillote with Vegetables

Salmon fillets atop fresh vegetables, wrapped in a parchment paper pouch and baked.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 Shallot
  • 2 Salmon fillets
  • 1 Potato
  • 1 lemon
  • A handful of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of Asparagus

Steps:

  1. Prepare a large sheet of baking paper, large enough for the two salmon fillets.
  2. After trimming the stems, lie the asparagus on the baking paper.
  3. Layer thinly sliced potato, then place the salmon fillet on top.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the minced garlic and sliced shallots on top of the fish.
  6. Place some cherry tomatoes around the sides.
  7. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil
  8. Take another sheet of baking paper, fold in the edges of both sheets to create a sealed in bag.
  9. Bake in the oven for 20 to 25 minutes (depending on the thickness of the fish) at 200 Celsius.
